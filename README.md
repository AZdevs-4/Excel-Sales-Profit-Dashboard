# Interactive Excel Sales & Profit Intelligence Dashboard

## 📝 Project Description
Retail businesses managing thousands of transactions across multiple years and regions often struggle to see where their revenue is concentrated. This Microsoft Excel dashboard turns raw multi-year transactional logs into an interactive, visual system that gives management instant answers about their performance.

## ❓ The Core Business Questions Answered
* **"Which regions and product sub-categories are driving our highest sales volume?"** 
* **"Who are our top 5 most valuable customers contributing to our $390,146 in total revenue?"**
* **"Are our monthly profit trends keeping pace with our sales spikes, or are margins dropping during specific seasons?"**

## ⚡ Technical Features (Built in Excel)
* **Dynamic Interactive Slicers:** Integrated multi-layered filters for Years (2021–2024), Months, Categories, and States.
* **Geographic Sales Mapping:** Built an interactive US map chart that automatically highlights high-performing states like California.
* **Dual-Line Trend Analysis:** Created a clean timeline chart tracking Sales and Profit simultaneously across months.
* **Automated Rankings:** Used Pivot Tables to dynamically rank sub-category sales and display the top 5 high-value customers.
